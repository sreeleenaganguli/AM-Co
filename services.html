Services


Audit and Assurance


We conduct detailed financial audits that go beyond mere compliance. Our in-depth analysis provides you with a clear understanding of your financial position, helping you make informed decisions and instilling confidence in stakeholders. Our team comprises of highly skilled and experienced auditors who bring a wealth of knowledge across various industries. Key industries that we’ve catered to include health sectors, entertainment sector, power and manufacturing, food & hospitality, mining, consumer durables, to name a few.

We provide the following Audit and Assurance Services


  • • Statutory Audits, Tax Audit, Review of Financial Statements and GST Audit
  • • Internal Audit
  • • Stock and Fixed Assets Audit
  • • CARO 2020 Advisory

Taxation Services

From filing accurate and timely returns to addressing specific industry-related tax considerations, we've got you covered.

Our services include consideration of the following laws and regulations:


  • • Direct Taxation
  • • GST Laws
  • • Central Sales Tax Laws
  • • Professional Taxation and State Value Added Tax
  • • Cross Border Tax Advisory and Transfer Pricing

Corporate Law & Secretarial Services


We understand the nuances of corporate governance, compliance, and transactions, ensuring that your business is in capable hands. MSME Consultation

We provide essential MSME advisory services, tailored to meet your everyday business needs. Our team of experienced professionals ensure that MSMEs can sail through the intricacies of the MSME sector.

  • • Assistance in the process of business registration and obtaining necessary licenses and permits.
  • • Ensuring compliance with local, regional, and national regulatory requirements.
  • • Providing guidance on financial planning, budgeting, and cash flow management.
