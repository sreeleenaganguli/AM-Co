About Us


A. Mukhopadhyay & Co, Chartered Accountants started its journey from July 1, 1950, and the strategy of the Firm has always been to offer the best of its services to its clients. The organization has been able to steer its way under the leadership of CA Animesh Mukhopadhyay, FCA, DISA (ICAI), Certificate Courses on MSME and CSR from ICAI, Qualified Insolvency Professional from IBBI along with other two senior partners CA Bimal Kumar Das, FCA and CA Manimay Nandi, FCA and a team of young and resourceful hands who through their repertoire of knowledge and experience have earned the confidence of clients.


A. MUKHOPADHYAY & CO is a seven decades old CAG empanelled firm, offering a boutique of financial services, ensuring that excellence meets expertise in navigating the intricate landscape of regulatory and legislative challenges. We don't just stay up-to-date; we lead the way. Our team of seasoned professionals possess a deep understanding of the ever-evolving accounting, auditing and other financial and commercial landscape, ensuring your business is not just compliant but strategically positioned for success.


Our firm caters to a wide range of diverse services ranging from Tax advisory and planning, Regulatory compliances, Insolvency laws, Mergers & Acquisitions to Auditing and Assurance services. Our comprehensive approach ensures that every financial aspect of your business is meticulously handled, providing you with peace of mind and the freedom to focus on your core operations.


We have consistently delivered exceptional results for our clients, navigating even the most complex issues with finesse. Our track record demonstrates our ability to turn challenges into triumphs. Each member of our TEAM brings a wealth of experience and expertise, contributing to a collective intelligence that sets us apart.


The firm is empanelled with RBI for conducting statutory audit of nationalized banks. The firm is empanelled with Comptroller and Auditors General of India.
